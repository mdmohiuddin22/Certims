MDQMS: ISO 13485:2016 (Medical Device Quality Management Systems)

Importance of ISO 13485 Certification for Medical Device Quality Management Systems (MDQMS)
ISO 13485:2016 specifies the requirements for a quality management system specific to medical devices. It ensures that organizations meet regulatory requirements and consistently produce high-quality medical devices.

ISO 13485 certification is crucial for your organization in the following ways:

  • It ensures compliance with regulatory requirements for medical devices.
  • It promotes a risk management approach to reduce product defects.
  • It enhances the safety and performance of medical devices.
  • It ensures traceability and accountability throughout the product lifecycle.

Benefits of ISO 13485 - Medical Device Quality Certification

  • Ensures compliance with international medical device regulations.
  • Enhances the safety and effectiveness of medical devices.
  • Reduces product recalls and the associated costs.
  • Builds customer trust by ensuring consistent product quality.
  • Facilitates access to global markets by meeting international standards.
