ISO Certification Services

What is ISO Certification?

ISO Certification ensures that your organization adheres to internationally recognized standards for quality, safety, efficiency, and environmental sustainability. At Certims Tech Solution (CTS), we guide businesses through the process of achieving ISO certifications, such as ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 22000, ISO 27001, ISO 20000-1, and ISO 13485:2016, which enhance operational performance, improve customer satisfaction, and strengthen market competitiveness.

Accreditation

Certims Tech Solution (CTS) offers fully accredited certificates that are recognized by buyers and organizations around the globe. Our certificates are endorsed with the logos of the Accreditation Body (AB), Certification Body (CB), and the International Accreditation Forum (IAF), ensuring worldwide acceptance and credibility.

CTS is an authorized International partner of AQC, a UAE-based certification body accredited by both the United Accreditation Foundation (UAF) and the Egyptian Accreditation Council (EGAC). If you're looking to obtain an accredited ISO certificate directly from the UAE, CTS is your ideal partner. We ensure that you receive globally recognized certifications backed by the credibility and authority of UAF and EGAC.
