Project Management

Our project management consultancy services focus on helping businesses plan, execute, and complete projects on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards. From managing risks to optimizing resources, CTS brings the expertise needed to ensure your projects succeed in today’s fast-paced business environment.

Industrial Automation

  • Certification: We offer certification services for industrial automation systems to ensure compliance with international standards. Our certification helps in validating the quality and reliability of automation solutions.
  • Development: Our team specializes in the development of industrial automation solutions tailored to specific industry needs. From concept to execution, we handle the entire development process to enhance operational efficiency.
  • Implementation: We provide end-to-end implementation services, including system integration, installation, and testing, ensuring seamless deployment of automation systems in your operations.

Business Consultancy

  • Startup Support: Our consultancy services offer comprehensive support to startups, including business planning, market analysis, and strategic guidance to help your business establish a strong foundation.
  • Business Development: We provide expert advice and strategies for business growth, including market expansion, operational improvements, and competitive positioning to drive long-term success.
  • Strategic Planning: Our consultancy includes developing strategic plans to align your business goals with actionable steps, ensuring efficient execution and sustainable growth.

Bid/Tender Management

  • Local Bid Management: We manage local bids and tenders, providing support with documentation, compliance, and submission to enhance your chances of winning contracts.
  • International Bid Management: Our expertise extends to international bid management, assisting with cross-border tenders, regulatory compliance, and strategic bid preparation for global opportunities.
  • Bid Strategy: We develop and execute bid strategies tailored to your business, ensuring competitive and compelling proposals that meet the requirements of potential clients.

International Procurement Management

  • Procurement Strategy: We design procurement strategies to optimize sourcing, supplier management, and cost control, ensuring efficient and effective procurement processes.
  • Supplier Selection: Our services include identifying and evaluating international suppliers, negotiating contracts, and managing supplier relationships to secure the best terms and quality.
  • Compliance and Risk Management: We ensure compliance with international trade regulations and manage risks associated with global procurement, safeguarding your business from potential challenges.
