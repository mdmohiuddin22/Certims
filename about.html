Who We Are

At Certims Tech Solution (CTS), we offer a comprehensive range of services, including ISO Certification, Compliance Management Certification, Assessment, Training, Project Management Consultancy, and Business Management Consultancy. Our expertise extends to advanced Software Development, Website Development, and other technical solutions designed to empower your business.

At CTS, we don’t just provide services—we deliver results. Our team of seasoned professionals is dedicated to guiding you through complex management systems and helping you achieve ISO certifications that set your business apart. We also offer in-depth training programs that equip your team with the skills needed to excel in today’s competitive market, ensuring your business meets global standards.

Our Compliance Management services cover key standards such as BSCI, Sedex, GMP, HACCP, and more, ensuring your operations meet global benchmarks for quality, safety, and ethical practices. In project management, our consultants bring extensive experience to ensure that your projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.

At CTS, we provide a robust suite of assessment services to help your organization achieve excellence, compliance, and operational efficiency. Our offerings include Compliance Assessment, Supplier/Vendor Assessment, Quality Management Assessment, Risk Management Assessment, GAP Analysis, and more. We use industry best practices to provide actionable insights tailored to your unique needs.

Technology is at the heart of everything we do. Whether it’s developing powerful software solutions, building dynamic websites, or offering cutting-edge technical solutions, CTS is committed to helping your business thrive in the digital age.

Our mission is simple: to empower businesses by delivering top-tier services and solutions that drive growth, ensure compliance, and enhance overall performance. We are passionate about what we do, and our success is defined by the success of our clients.
